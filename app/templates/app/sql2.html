Welcome to the data display page. Below, you will find several tables showcasing various datasets, including suppliers, warehouse products, customers, orders, and order details. All the data presented here has been generated using random text and date functions for demonstration purposes. Each table represents a different aspect of my mock database:

  • Suppliers: Information about suppliers including their contact details and addresses.
  • Warehouse Products: Details of products stored in the warehouse, their categories, quantities, and prices.
  • Customers: Customer data, including their names, contact information, and addresses.
  • Orders: Order records, including customer details, order dates, shipping information, and freight costs.
  • Order Details: Specific details for each order, including the products ordered, quantities, unit prices, and any discounts applied.
